👯 2 Player Games

WoopKid World › 2 Player Games

Looking for a two player game the kids can play together right now, on one screen, with zero setup? This is our same-screen battle shelf. No ads, no accounts, no downloads: open a game, hand over half the keyboard (or half the touch screen) and start playing.

Bubble Tank Duel is the star of the shelf. One player drives with WASD and shoots with E, the other uses the arrow keys and Space, and on tablets each player simply drags on their own half of the screen. Bubble shots bounce off the maze walls, so every round turns into giggly chaos. First to 5 pops wins bragging rights. There is also a solo mode against a very silly robot if no second player is around.

We are a small, dad-built site, so this category is honest about its size: one great head-to-head game today, with more same-screen battles already in the works. Check back soon, or try the action and puzzle shelves while you wait.
