🧩 Puzzle Games

WoopKid World › Puzzle Games

These are our thinking games: the ones where a little planning beats fast fingers. All four run free in the browser with no ads, no accounts and nothing to install, so they work great on a school Chromebook or a family tablet.

Merge Drop is a gentle physics puzzle where matching animals combine into bigger ones, all the way up to a legendary Unicorn. Color Pop is a classic bubble shooter with wall bounces and big satisfying cascades. Block Stax is our take on falling block puzzles: slide, spin and stack seven pieces and try to clear four lines at once. Sky Stack is all timing, slicing your tower thinner with every slightly-off drop.

Every game saves your best score on your own device, so kids can chase their own records without anyone making a profile. They are gentle enough for younger kids to enjoy and tricky enough to keep older ones hooked. Pick a card below and start puzzling.
