For teachers

Indoor recess and brain breaks without the ad minefield. Built by a parent, free forever.

Why teachers use WoopKid

  • Zero ads and zero trackers, so nothing inappropriate can appear next to a game. Ever.
  • No accounts and no chat: students cannot be contacted, and nothing is collected (COPPA-friendly, see privacy).
  • Runs on Chromebooks and tablets straight from the browser. No downloads, no installs, fast on weak hardware.
  • Short rounds (30 seconds to a few minutes) that fit a brain break, with a natural stopping point at each game over.

For your IT admin: allowlist details

  • Domain to allow: woopkid.com (plus fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com, usually already allowed)
  • Static HTML/CSS/JavaScript only. No iframes to third parties, no ad exchanges, no user-generated content, no chat, no external links inside games.
  • Suggested category: Games / Education. The site carries no advertising category triggers.

Questions from your district's filtering team are welcome: the entire site is open source at github.com/Ronkimhi/woopkid-games, so they can inspect every line.
