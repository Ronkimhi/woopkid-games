This is the fast shelf: runners, dodgers, jumpers and one platformer that openly cheats. Everything here is free, browser-based and honestly ad-free, so kids can jump straight into the action on a Chromebook, tablet or phone with nothing to install.

Trap Land is the troll platformer: 12 handcrafted levels where floors vanish and doors run away, and beating them anyway feels amazing. Road Hopper and Tap Rush are one-tap runners that are easy to start and hard to put down. Speed Slope and Drift & Collect bring the speed, Noodle Arena throws you into a snake battle against 12 silly rivals, and Beat Bounce turns jumping into a rhythm game.

Most of these need just one finger or one key, so younger kids can join in too, while the rising speed keeps older kids coming back for one more run. Best scores save right on your own device, no profile needed. Pick your favorite below and go.
